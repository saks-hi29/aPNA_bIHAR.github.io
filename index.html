Tradition

The traditional dress of Bihari people consists of dhoti-kurta for men and saree for women.

Festival

Chhath puja is the most famous and original celebrated festival among the many fairs and festivals of Bihar. It is an ancient Hindu festival in the worship of 'Surya', the sun deity

Cuisine

Litti Chokha, a baked salted wheat-flour cake filled with sattu (baked chickpea flour) and some special spices, which is served with baigan bharta, made of roasted eggplant (brinjal) and tomatoes.

Temples

The Bihar Temples are Known for architectural brilliance made by by differnt emperors and kings in different eras.

Art forms

Tikuli is a type of hand painting and its history spread over 800 years. The painting with its modern evolution and varied manifestations, find its origin in Patna, Bihar.

Language

There are many languages spoken in Bihar.Out of all chief among them are Bhojpuri, spoken in the west of the state, Maithili in the north, and Magahi in the south..
